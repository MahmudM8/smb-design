Our Heritage

SMB DESIGN was founded with a deep passion for showcasing African craftsmanship to the world.

Each pair of shoes tells a unique story — of the skilled hands that shape them, the cultures that inspire them, and the individuals who wear them with pride.

Our footwear is carefully handcrafted, blending age-old techniques passed down through generations with modern design aesthetics. We use only the finest materials — from ethically-sourced leathers to intricately handwoven textiles.

Every stitch, every bead, every gold detail is crafted with purpose, creating not just shoes, but a tribute to the rich and timeless heritage of African design.
